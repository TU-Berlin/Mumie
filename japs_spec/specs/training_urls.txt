                           URLS DES TRAININGS-TOOLS


1. Problem-Element zum Bearbeiten:

     protected/document/training/type-name/element/id/ID

   Request-Parameter:     

     ref=REF

2. Download eines Input-Data-Sheets:

     protected/training/download/problem-input-datasheet

   Request-Parameter:

     ref=REF

3. Upload eines Antwort-Data-Sheets:

     protected/training/upload/problem-answer-datasheet

   Request-Parameter:

     ref=REF

     content=DATA_SHEET

4. Upload von Multiple-Choice-Antworten:

     protected/training/upload/multiple-choice-answers

   Request-Parameter:

     ref=REF


Abkürzungen:

REF 
    Id der course_subsection-element-Referenz

DATA_SHEET 
    Code des Data-Sheets, URL-encoded
